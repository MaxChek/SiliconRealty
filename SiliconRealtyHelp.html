Frequently Asked Questions


Top FAQs for Renters


  • Does Silicon Realty screen listings that are showcased?

    • In order to create a safe marketplace and ensure the accuracy of listings on the Silicon Realty network, we verify all listings submitted through the Add a Property feature, with the exclusion of third party feeds.

  • How do I apply to rentals?

    • Searching for a new place can be tough, but finding your perfect place is possible! Applying to rentals through Apartments.com is easy and just takes a few minutes. For $29.00 (plus tax), you can apply to 10 participating listings for up to 30 days. Fill out your application once, and we'll save your info in case you want to apply to more places.

  • My lease signing was canceled. What do I do next?

    • All parties on the lease, including landlords and co-landlords, have until 11:59 PM on the lease start date to finalize signing the lease. If the lease was not signed by all lease parties by the lease start date, the lease signing will automatically be cancelled.

  • A property manager is not responding, what do I do?

    • Listings on Silicon Realty are self-managed by the property manager, owner or agent, and we're sorry we're not able to help here. If a property manager is not answering the phone, returning a call, or responding to an email, you may want to move on to another rental that interests you.

Top FAQs for Property Owners


  • How do I edit the information about my home?

    • To edit your home facts (like bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, and other details), you must claim ownership of your home's property page and navigate to the Sell page.

  • Where does Silicon Realty get information about my property?

    • Silicon Realty receives information about property sales from the municipal office responsible for recording real estate transactions in your area. The information we provide is public information gathered from county records. Our parcel information, which outlines the lot on which a house sits, comes from various public sources, such as the county. We regularly update this information as we receive it from data providers.

  • How do I add or remove photos of my home?

    • To add or remove photos on your home's property page, you must claim ownership of your home's property page and navigate to the Sell page.

  • How do I report an incorrect address for my home?

    • If you notice a home or listing is displaying an incorrect address, you can report it to our team.
