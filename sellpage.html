Sell Your Home Yourself

For-sale-by-owner or (FSBO) is referred to when you decide to sell your home on your own.
This process is similar to traditional selling, but without the assistance of a real estate agenet.
As a result you will be responsible for preparing the home, marketing, showing, and price negotiation.

Why sell FSBO?

Avoid paying commission to an agent. Advertise your listings for free. Control, Flexibility from start to finish.

How to sell FSBO

When selling on your own, begin by preparing your home, as well as hiring a professional photographer. Once you gather all your marketing materials, you may begin to research comparable homes to find the suitable price. Lastly, list on Silicon Realty. You will most likely host home showings or open houses, decide on an offer, negotiate, accept and close.
